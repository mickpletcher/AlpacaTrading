## Learning Roadmap (Parallel to Using These Tools)

**Month 1 — Foundation**
- Read both books: *How to Day Trade for a Living* (Aziz) + *How to Day Trade: The Plain Truth*
- Set up paper trading account
- Run backtester on 3-5 different stocks and timeframes
- Start journaling hypothetical trades (no real trades yet)

**Month 2 — Paper Trade**
- Trade paper every day the market is open
- Log every trade in the journal — setup, emotion, mistake
- Run AI analysis weekly to spot patterns
- Focus on 1-2 setups max (EMA crossover or VWAP)

**Month 3 — Review**
- Review journal. If win rate > 50% and profit factor > 1.5 — consider real money
- Start with 1 share per trade — focus on execution, not P&L
- Keep position size at max 1% risk per trade

**Real Money Minimum**
- $25,000 account (PDT requirement for unlimited day trades)
- OR futures account (no PDT, trade ES/NQ micro contracts with ~$500 margin)
- OR cash account (no PDT, but trades settle T+2 so capital is tied up)
